## 2. Logo

**Mark:** a fine-line mountain ridge (peaks) enclosed in a thin gold ring, resting on a horizontal "signature" baseline. It expresses Alpine + a signed seal of quality.

**Lockups**
- **Stacked (primary):** mark above the wordmark set on three lines (ALPINE / SIGNATURE / LIVING), with a short gold rule and the descriptor beneath.
- **Horizontal:** mark left, a thin divider rule, then wordmark + category line — for headers, letterhead, email signatures.
- **Reversed:** same lockups in stone/paper on the Alpine Green background.
- **Monogram:** capital **A** (Cormorant Garamond) inside the gold ring — for avatars, favicons, social.

**Construction & usage**
- Wordmark is always uppercase, letter-spaced ~0.13–0.18em.
- Single-colour version: entire mark in Alpine Green (or pure black) for stamps, fax, engraving.
- Clear space: keep a margin of at least the ring's diameter ÷ 2 around the logo.
- Minimum size: emblem legible down to ~24px; below that, drop the baseline rule.
- Do not: recolour outside the palette, add gradients/shadows, stretch, rotate, or set the wordmark in a different typeface.

## 3. Colour palette

| Role | Name | HEX | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary / ink | **Alpine Green** | `#1E2A23` | Backgrounds, wordmark, body text on light |
| Accent | **Signature Gold** | `#B89160` | Ring, rules, small accents only — never large fills |
| Secondary | **Slate Sage** | `#6F7D74` | Descriptors, captions, secondary text |
| Surface | **Stone** | `#F4F1EC` | Warm off-white background |
| Surface | **Paper** | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, documents |

**Accent variants** (optional, pick one and stay consistent): Gold `#B89160` · Copper `#B07A52` · Sage `#6F8A6E` · Slate `#5F7384`.

Usage ratio: ~70% Stone/Paper, ~22% Alpine Green, ~8% Signature Gold. Gold is a jewel accent — use sparingly.

## 4. Typography

| Use | Typeface | Weights | Treatment |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Display / wordmark / headlines** | **Cormorant Garamond** | 400–600, italic for emphasis | Elegant serif; large sizes; the italic carries the slogan |
| **Supporting / descriptors / body / UI** | **Jost** | 300–500 | Geometric sans; uppercase + wide letter-spacing (0.22–0.34em) for labels and descriptors; sentence case for body |

- Headlines: Cormorant Garamond 500.
- Slogan: Cormorant Garamond 500 *italic*.
- Labels/descriptors: Jost 300, uppercase, letter-spacing ~0.3em.
- Body: Jost 300/400, sentence case, line-height ~1.6–1.9.
- Both are free Google Fonts:
  `https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Cormorant+Garamond:ital,wght@0,400;0,500;0,600;1,400;1,500&family=Jost:wght@300;400;500&display=swap`

## 6. Voice & tone

Speak the way the brand looks: measured, warm, and exact. Short, confident sentences. Lead with the client's project, not the company. Avoid hype, exclamation marks, and jargon. Swiss precision without coldness — the founder is a single, accountable point of contact, and the language should feel personal and reassuring.
