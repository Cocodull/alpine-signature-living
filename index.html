About

"Owning a residence in Valais should be a source of pleasure — not a burden of complexity. I exist to ensure it remains exactly that."

I am Corinne Dulles, founder of Alpine Signature Living. My professional background spans senior roles in global companies — working with Fortune 500 clients on strategic consulting, business transformation, and data-driven analytics. I bring that same rigour to every property project: structured coordination, precise budget management, and a trusted network of architects, contractors, and financial professionals built through years of local relationships.

Whether you are in London, Geneva, or Singapore, I act as your eyes, your voice, and your advocate — from the first planning submission to the final handover. My approach is personal, meticulous, and entirely confidential.

Services

Acquisition Advisory

Sourcing and evaluating properties that meet your criteria — from alpine chalets to investment residences — through a carefully cultivated network of local agents, notaries, and private contacts.

Construction & Renovation

End-to-end coordination of new builds and renovations — from contractor selection and permit applications to site supervision and final handover. Your project delivered on time and within budget.

Administrative & Permit Coordination

All correspondence, permit applications, notarial procedures, and local authority requirements handled with precision — in French and in English. Nothing overlooked.

Specification of interior finishes, material selections, and furnishing — from tile and flooring choices to kitchen design and final staging. A considered interior that reflects the quality of the project.
